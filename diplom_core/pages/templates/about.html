About us

Welcome to our store, where tradition and modern style meet in every pot.

We are a team inspired by the philosophy of Feng Shui, creating unique clay pots that help fill your home with harmony and tranquility. Our mission is to bring natural energy and beauty into every corner of your home.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring the natural energy and beauty into every corner of your home, using ancient Feng Shui knowledge in modern design. We believe that our surroundings have a profound impact on our inner state. Every product in our collection is created to help you create a harmonious and peaceful space.

Our Values

  • **Harmony and Balance**: We strive to create products that promote harmony and balance in your home.
  • **Quality**: Each of our products is crafted with special attention to detail from high-quality, eco-friendly materials.
  • **Aesthetics**: Our collection of clay pots with soft shades and a variety of shapes perfectly fits into any interior.

Our History

Our journey began with a simple desire to share the beauty and tranquility that Feng Shui provides. We carefully studied ancient traditions and combined them with modern design to create a collection that is not only beautiful but also beneficial for your home.

FAQs

Here are answers to the most common questions from our customers.

What are your delivery times?

Our standard delivery time is 3-7 working days, depending on your location. You will receive a tracking number as soon as your order is dispatched.

How can I return a product?

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 14 days of receipt. The product must be in its original condition. Please contact our support team to arrange the return.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and PayPal. All payments are securely processed.

From what materials are your vases made?

Our vases are handcrafted from high-quality, eco-friendly clay. We use natural pigments and glazes to ensure the product's safety and durability.
